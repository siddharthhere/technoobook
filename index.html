Welcome to techo books

Techo Books specialises in online sales of specialised technical publications and antique books.

In particular we specialise in books in computing and mathematics.

Because we don't run a show room, we are able to keep our prices down. All our books are in good condition.

Our online showroom is divided into two parts - those for the computing books, and those for mathematical texts. We think you'll like our range.
